DREAMHEAD CITIZEN

a museum of consciousness...

Dyami Damon Quay Clemente
I have always been a Surrealist, a vagabond, seeking an absolute reality through the marshes of the metaphysical.
That of which grants its being out of the marriage of the conscious and subconscious, of reality and surreality.
To me, my art is a visionary translator to this metaphysical reality that each of us store elusively within...
One could even call this place of being... Soul.
